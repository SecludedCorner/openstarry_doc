# 68. MR-11: Tenets as Architectural Choice

> **決定權限**: Master permanent ruling (MR-11), communicated during R2-R3 transition。
> **規則**: MR-11 (permanent), connected to MR-1, MR-7, MR-8, MR-10。

---

## 1. MR-11 Statement

> 「十大宣言是 OpenStarry 的架構，也是核心價值。十大宣言是一種選擇，符合十大宣言的架構，本身就是一種抉擇。」

"The Ten Tenets are OpenStarry's architecture and its core values. The Ten Tenets are a choice; an architecture that conforms to the Ten Tenets is itself a decision."

---

## 2. Context

MR-11 was established during Cycle 03-7 when COND-4 (coldStartGear) was initially classified as a "boundary issue" — technically compliant but with a minor gap. This treated the tenets as a checklist. Master's response reframed the paradigm: the tenets are not external rules imposed on the codebase — they ARE the codebase's identity. Conforming to them is fidelity to a chosen identity, not box-checking.

---

## 3. Meaning

- **Tenets = architecture**: Every architectural decision is an expression of the tenets, not separate from them
- **Tenets = deliberate choice**: The word "choice" (選擇/抉擇) appears twice, emphasizing deliberateness. They were chosen as identity, not imposed by necessity
- **Compliance = fidelity**: Meeting the tenets is being faithful to a chosen identity
- **"Works but doesn't honor the choice" = incomplete practice**: Even if technically passing D2 Predicate

### Two Questions, Not One

| Paradigm | Question | Evaluates |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Old (D2 v1 only) | Does it satisfy the D2 Predicate? | Technical completeness |
| New (D2 + MR-11) | Is it faithful to the architectural choice? | Identity fidelity |

Both must be asked; neither is sufficient alone.

---

## 5. Relationship to Existing MRs

| MR | Content | MR-11 Interpretation |
|----|---------|---------------------|
| MR-1 | Standards, not aspirational | Standards BECAUSE they are chosen values |
| MR-7 | Code complete = COMPLIANT | Code must embody the choice, not merely exist |
| MR-8 | Quality first | Quality = fidelity to the choice |
| MR-10 | Fabrication must be repaired | Known departures from identity are repaired, not rationalized |

Under MR-11, the permanent rulings form a coherent philosophy: chosen identity (MR-11) -> standard (MR-1) -> code embodies identity (MR-7) -> unconditional commitment (MR-8) -> departures repaired (MR-10).

---

## 6. Application Rule

MR-11 applies always: every cycle (evaluate fidelity, not just compliance), every phase (trace decisions to tenets), every debate (reference faithfulness to the chosen identity). This principle is the foundation upon which all other rules stand — the "why" behind every "what."
